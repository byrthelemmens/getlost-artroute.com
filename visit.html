Because the art route is in the public domain, the exhibition is open every day of the week, day or night. Entrance if for free. As all artworks are fragile we kindly ask you not to touch the works.

The art route is very easy to reach by bike, public transport and car. The Zuidas is approximately 15 minutes by bike from the centre of Amsterdam. Station Amsterdam Zuid (metro 51, tram 5 several trans from and to Schiphol Airport) is located on the art route. By car, from the A10 Ring road Zuid you take junction s108.
