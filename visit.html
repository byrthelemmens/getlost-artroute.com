Because the art route is in the public domain, the exhibition is open every day of the week, day or night. Entrance is for free. As all artworks are fragile. We kindly ask you not to touch the works.

Bicycle

The Zuidas is approximately 15 minutes by bike from the centre of Amsterdam.

Public Transport

Station Amsterdam Zuid Metro 51 Tram 5

By Car

The Zuidas is on the Amsterdam Ring A10 Zuid, junction s108.
