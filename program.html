The first GET LOST - art route will take place between 12 June and 25 September 2015 in the Zuidas area of Amsterdam. The Zuidas (literally ‘South Axis’) is Amsterdam’s international knowledge and financial district and lies between the centre of Amsterdam and Schiphol airport. Seven companies and organisations have commissioned artworks from eight artists for the public space of the Zuidas.

GET LOST - art route generates art in public space through partnering businesses with young artists. This creates a meeting place – the art route. During GET LOST - art route 2015, members of the public will be able to get lost in the Zuidas as it wanders from artwork to artwork.

GET LOST - art route is organising a number of public events aimed at bringing about interactions between people who work in the Zuidas, artists, students and residents.
